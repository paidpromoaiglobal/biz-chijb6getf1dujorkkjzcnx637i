About Us

Saibaba Tuitions and Activities Center

Saibaba Tuitions and Activities Center is a trusted learning hub located in Soumya Nagar, Medavakkam, Chennai. Under the dedicated guidance of Mahalakshmi Ma'am and her experienced faculty, the center offers personalized academic tuitions and enriching skill-based activities designed to help every child — even the weakest student — achieve their full potential. With a perfect 5-star rating, the center has earned the love and trust of families across the neighborhood.

From subject-focused tuition classes in Tamil and other core subjects to engaging programs like Phonics and memorable Summer Camps, Saibaba Tuitions and Activities Center goes beyond textbooks to make learning fun and effective. The center operates six days a week from 4:30 PM to 7:30 PM, making it perfectly convenient for school-going children. Parents consistently praise the center for its caring approach, skilled faculty, and the visible improvements in their children's academic performance and confidence.
